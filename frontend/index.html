Our Story

KishansKraft was born from a simple belief - everyone deserves access to pure, healthy oils the way nature intended. We partner with local farmers who have been growing mustard and other oil seeds for generations, using traditional methods that have been passed down through families.

Our cold-pressing process uses wooden presses, just like our ancestors did. This method ensures that the oils retain their natural nutrients, flavor, and therapeutic properties. No chemicals, no heat, no compromise - just pure, authentic oil from farm to your kitchen.
